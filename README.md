**Project Overview**

This project analyzes the sales performance of a gifting company using Excel Pivot Tables and Pivot Charts. The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, delivery experience, and regional demand to support business decision‑making.
The objective of this analysis is to help stakeholders understand what drives sales, which occasions and products perform best, and how delivery experience impacts customer satisfaction.

**Problem Statement**

Gifting businesses experience strong seasonality, varying customer demand across occasions, and high dependency on delivery timelines. The challenge is to:

Identify key revenue‑generating occasions and products
Understand customer behavior across regions
Evaluate delivery performance and its impact on customer satisfaction
Improve operational efficiency and sales growth

**Data Source**: Kaggle Dataset.


**Tools Used**

Microsoft Excel,
Pivot Tables,
Pivot Charts,
Slicers & Filters,
DAX,
Dashboard Design Techniques


**Key Insights**

The company generated approximately $3.5M in total revenue with 3,500+ orders.
Sales show strong seasonal patterns, driven mainly by festivals and personal occasions.
Anniversaries, birthdays, and festivals like Holi contribute the highest revenue.
Revenue is concentrated in a small set of top‑performing gift products, indicating strong customer preference for curated gift packs.
A few cities dominate order volume, while many regions show potential for growth.
Delivery time plays a critical role in customer satisfaction, with most customers preferring deliveries within 3–5 days.
Extremely slow deliveries negatively impact customer behavior, especially for time‑sensitive gifting occasions.


**Business Recommendations**

Focus marketing efforts on high‑performing personal occasions such as anniversaries and birthdays.
Expand express and reliable delivery options during peak gifting seasons.
Promote top‑selling products and bundle slower‑moving items to increase average order value.
Improve logistics performance to maintain consistent 3–5 day delivery timelines.
Target high‑order cities with region‑specific promotions while expanding reach in emerging markets.


**Dashboard Outcome**

The dashboard enables business stakeholders to:
Track overall sales performance
Identify peak occasions and products
Understand customer behavior and delivery expectations
Make data‑driven decisions to improve revenue and customer experience
